Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Pixelcon Word Guesser collects and uses information when you use the app.

2. Information We Collect

The app collects and stores:

* A unique user identifier generated through Firebase Authentication.
* Game progress and game-related data.
* Information about in-app purchases and purchase restoration.
* Technical information required for the operation of the app.

The app does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

3. Why We Process Your Data

We process your data to:

* Provide and maintain the app.
* Save and restore game progress.
* Restore purchases across devices.
* Protect the app from abuse and fraud.
* Provide customer support.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses services provided by:

* Google Firebase (user identification)
* Google Play Billing (processing and verification of in-app purchases)

These services may process data on our behalf in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Legal Basis (EU Users)

For users in the European Economic Area, personal data is processed because it is necessary to provide the services requested by the user.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide the app services.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

* Access your personal data.
* Correct inaccurate data.
* Request deletion of your personal data.
* Object to certain processing activities.

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

8. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at doublestonestudios@gmx.at

Upon verification of the request, personal data associated with your account will be deleted unless retention is required by law.

9. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact doublestonestudios@gmx.at.
